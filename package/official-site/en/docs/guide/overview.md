# Overview

TrailSnap is a **photo album application** focused on "travel photos and itinerary memories". Its core goal is to transform photos from a pile of files into a **data center** that is searchable, statistically analyzable, and reviewable, preserving all your **travel memories**.

## In a Nutshell

- You provide the photo directory, and TrailSnap handles indexing, previews, retrieval, and visualization.
- The backend manages media/tasks/statistics/tickets, while AI microservices provide recognition capabilities.
- The frontend Web application is for daily use, and the official website is for documentation and demos.

## Key Paths (Recommended Experience Order)

- Step 1: Get it running by following the [Installation Guide](/en/docs/guide/install).
- Step 2: Import photos and trigger indexing/tasks (generate previews, extract metadata).
- Step 3: Experience AI capabilities (OCR/Face Recognition/Classification/Ticket Recognition).
- Step 4: Review your travel footprints with statistics and annual reports.
