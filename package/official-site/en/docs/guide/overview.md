# Overview 🚀

TrailSnap is an intelligent **AI photo album** application dedicated to helping users easily record, organize, and review their travel experiences. Through powerful AI processing capabilities, every photo and every journey becomes a memory worth cherishing.

In the future, everyone (at least every family) will have their own AI data center, and the photo album is an important data source for the data center. It preserves many moments in your life. AI photo albums transform these moments into valuable memories. It can silently record tickets and scenic spot tickets in your album, help you record what you see and hear during travel, automatically organize photos that can be posted to social media (and even prepare captions for you), and help you cut a 15-second short video...

## ✨ Key Features

### 🖼️ Standard Album

- Traditional photo album functions, supporting uploading, managing, and viewing photos
- Supports **Live Photos** from mobile phones such as iPhone, vivo, Xiaomi, OPPO, etc.

### 🤖 Smart Album

- OCR recognition
- Face recognition
- Intelligent scene classification
- Smart search (supports searching for text, faces, categories, and image content in photos)
- Conditional albums (supports automatically generating albums based on time, location, person, category, image content, etc.)

### 🗺️ Footprint Album

- View photos on a map
- Record lit-up cities (supports provinces, cities, districts, and counties)
- Display visited 5A-level scenic spots (supports adding custom scenic spots or locations)

### 🎫 Ticket Management

- Automatically recognize ticket information in photos (such as train tickets, plane tickets, etc.)
- Support adding, managing, and viewing travel tickets
- Support viewing and exporting simulated paper train tickets (supports exporting to png format, with optional red and blue styles)

### 📊 [Annual Report](https://siyuan.ink/annual-report)

- Photo walls, photo calendars, visited cities, farthest cities visited and photos taken, photos of different seasons, best photos of the year, etc.

## 🛠️ Upcoming Features

- Toolbox
  - Image editing (supports cropping, rotating, adding text, adding tags, etc.)
  - Identify duplicate and similar photos
  - Automatically identify low-quality and meaningless photos
  - Image folder organization (categorize images into different folders by time, location, etc.)
- AI Feature Enhancement
  - Integrate AI large models to achieve more intelligent image classification, search, and recommendation functions
  - Automatically generate travel logs (supports automatically generating travel records based on photos, including itinerary, accommodation, food, people, scenery, etc.)
  - Use the photo album as a local database for intelligent dialogue
    - Where did I go this National Day?
    - Organize the photos from the weekend and help me prepare a nine-grid photo set and caption for social media.
